Who are We?

WE HELP MID-SIZED AND LARGE COMPANIES DESIGN, MANAGE AND MAINTAIN THEIR IT INFRUSTRUCTURE

WebSavvy evolved into an advanced solutions provider with an objective is to fully understand its customers business processes and to deliver significant added value to its customers by developing solutions that dramatically reduce their costs and enhance their ability to adapt to changes in their markets. We strive to deliver more efficient, effective and relevant quality services and solutions tailored to the increasingly complex demands of organizations, in order to boost productivity of operations and to maximize value for our customers.

Vision

To be a leading and self-sustaining Centre of Excellence for ICT capacity in Africa.

Mission

To provide quality training, consultancy and advisory services to the ICT Sector in Africa.
